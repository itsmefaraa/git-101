Fluffy Pancakes
===============

Makes about 8 pancakes.

Ingredients
-----------
- 1 cup plain flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps
-----
1. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Pour the wet mix into the dry mix and stir until just combined.
4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
5. Pour a small ladle of batter for each pancake.
6. Flip when bubbles appear on top, then cook 1 more minute.
7. Serve warm with your favourite topping.
